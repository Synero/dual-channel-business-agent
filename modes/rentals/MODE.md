# Mode: Rentals

**For:** Businesses that rent out physical items for a period of time.

## Examples
- Equipment rentals (cameras, audio, construction tools)
- Car rentals, bike rentals, scooter rentals
- Property rentals (vacation homes, apartments, offices)
- Costume/tuxedo rentals, dress rentals
- Camping gear, sports equipment
- Party supplies, furniture, AV equipment

## Customer Intent
The customer wants to:
1. Know what's available and the rental rates
2. Reserve an item for specific dates
3. Understand pickup/return logistics
4. Know deposit requirements and damage policies

## Conversation Flow
```
Customer: "Hola, alquilan cámaras?"
→ Greet briefly, confirm, list models with daily/weekly rates

Customer: "Necesito una del 15 al 20 de mayo"
→ Check availability for those dates, confirm model

Customer: "Cuánto sería?"
→ Calculate total (daily rate × days), mention deposit

Customer: "Dale, la quiero"
→ Confirm reservation, ask for pickup time, explain return policy

Customer: "La devuelvo el 20 a las 18:00"
→ Confirm, remind of deposit hold, send location for pickup
```

## Key Data
- **Inventory:** Items available, specs, conditions, photos
- **Availability Calendar:** Which items are booked on which dates
- **Reservations:** Active bookings with dates, customer, total, deposit
- **Customer Memory:** Rental history, preferred items, reliability score

## Payment Patterns
- Full payment upfront + refundable deposit
- Deposit only to reserve, balance on pickup
- Daily/weekly/monthly rates
- Late return fees

## What Makes This Mode Unique
- **Time-bound inventory** — same item can't be rented twice on same day
- **Condition matters** — item state before/after rental
- **Logistics-heavy** — pickup, return, cleaning between rentals
- **Deposit psychology** — customer worries about losing deposit
